Current Trends

Right now

Entering into a new decade the fashion of the 2020s has revived a lot of old trends, nitpicking from almost every era. The ones that stand out are the 2000's, 90's, 60's and 80's. The internet allows individuals to take inspiration from other people, time periods, and cultures. Fashion lines are blurring like never before making it all about self-expression.


In the age of social media, platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Pinterest have very fashion-heavy content. Many brands work with these websites and their influencers to promote and sell their products. Online stores such as Depop and Etsy allow for the sale of vintage, used, or homemade products. Within these platforms, many fashion aesthetics have been popularized like cottage core, VSCO, E-kid, indie, kid-core, academia and fairy grunge.


Runway and high fashion are still prevalent and have become increasingly popular over the years with events like the MET gala, and fashion weeks around the world broadcasting to millions of people. A lot of styles are unisex or genderless. This permits individuals of all identities to stray from the social norm and mix and match pieces of all ideals. Some trendsetters in androgynous fashion include Harry styles, Lil Nas X, K-pop body bands, Kristen Stewart, Tilda Swinton, and Cara Delevingne.




Popular hairstyles include shaggy haircuts, big voluminous curls, braids, twists, buzzcuts, bobs, and curtain bangs.
