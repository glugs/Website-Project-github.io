Fashion

What is it?

There are many interpretations as to what defines fashion. From mere garments to tools of empowerment, fashion is apart of your daily life whether you realize or not. What you choose to put on every day says a lot about you and how you want to present yourself to the world. Not only limited to clothing, fashion has served as a form of self exrpession and autonomy, driven by the desire for the new, as it constantly evolves.


About

Fashion, being a social and cultural phenomenom, has given rise to distincitve styles and forms of self expression throughout history. On this website-which generally focuses on womens wear-you'll be able to dip your toes in a condensed explanation(as there is so much information) of a few fashion categories and eras that I find interesting :).
